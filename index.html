About Sigma Credit Hire

Sigma Credit Hire Ltd provides professional credit hire and accident management services across Bolton, Greater Manchester, and beyond. We specialise in providing like-for-like replacement vehicles to drivers who have been involved in non-fault accidents.

Our experienced team handles everything from the initial claim through to vehicle return, working directly with insurers and solicitors to ensure a smooth, stress-free experience. When you're not at fault, you shouldn't be left without transport—that's where we come in.

Whether you need a standard vehicle, taxi, or specialist transport, we're here to keep you moving while your claim is processed.
