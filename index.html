About Us

Sigma Credit Hire Ltd provides professional credit hire and accident management services. We specialise in providing replacement vehicles to drivers who have been involved in non-fault accidents.

Our experienced team works directly with insurers and solicitors to ensure a smooth, hassle-free experience for our clients during what can be a stressful time.
